## Full Description (Both stores)

TextCoach helps you craft the perfect text message for any conversation — dating, professional, social, or personal.

Paste a conversation and get 2-4 ready-to-send message options, each with a different tone and approach. Every suggestion comes with an explanation of WHY it works, so you actually become a better communicator over time.

HOW IT WORKS:

Quick Suggest — Paste a conversation and instantly get multiple response options with different tones (playful, direct, casual, confident). Each comes with reasoning explaining why it works.

Coach Me — Answer a few contextual questions about the situation, and get even more tailored suggestions based on your goals and desired tone.

Conversation Dynamics — See what's really happening in your conversation: interest level, tone, patterns, and subtext decoded.

FEATURES:
• 2-4 text message suggestions per request
• Multiple tones: playful, direct, warm, casual, confident, flirty
• "Why this works" reasoning for every suggestion
• Conversation dynamics analysis
• Works for dating, professional, social, family conversations
• Screenshot OCR — take a screenshot and we'll read it
• Keyboard extension for inline suggestions
• Copy to clipboard with one tap
• Your conversations are never stored

KEYBOARD EXTENSION:
Install the TextCoach keyboard for quick suggestions without leaving your messaging app. Paste context, tap Suggest, and insert a response directly into the text field.

PRIVACY FIRST:
Your conversations are processed and immediately discarded. We never store message content. Screenshots are analyzed on-device — images never leave your phone.
